INTRODUCTION

     Al Tayyar Aluminum & Steel has its headquarters in Abu Dhabi, UAE. Established on the 3rd of July 2011, Al Tayyar Aluminum & Steel has been recognized as one of the area’s most experienced companies in shaping a new trend of modernizing building façades in the United Arab Emirates.

     Today, Al Tayyar Aluminum & Steel takes on the role of main contractor for small to large size projects and performs with its best consultants, engineers, designers and administrative experts that are current on the latest technology and performs value-added services, capable of responding to client’s requirements and meeting their expectations. With our highly rated quality aluminum and steel products, providing ISO Certified services in installation, maintenance systems and support, we are able to help property developers, architects and building owners renew and transform the façade / exterior envelop of buildings. We focus on streamlining the process of aluminum installation works, providing feasibility and develop- ment for refurbishment of existing cladding as well as re-cladding and over cladding of existing offices, towers, buildings as well as villas.

     Al Tayyar Aluminum & Steel is unique in providing specialized Façade Consultancy and not just changing outer building appearances, it also focuses on sustainability.

VISION & VALUES

     To be a leader in façade consulting, metal and iron works, aluminum installation on a truly global scale and lead the market trend with constant innovation and be among the most sustainable firms in the world. Our employees flourish to the best of their abil- ities and we take responsibility for people and the environment with sustainable products.
